## Overview

Trackora's Fleet Management System is the command centre for your entire vehicle operation. Whether you manage 10 trucks or 10,000, every asset is tracked, measured, and optimised from a single pane of glass.

## Key Features

- **Live GPS Tracking** — 5-second refresh intervals with real-time map view
- **Driver Management** — Assign drivers to vehicles, track shifts, and monitor behaviour
- **Geo-fencing** — Define zones and receive instant alerts on entry/exit violations
- **Trip History & Replay** — Full route replay with speed, stop, and idling overlays
- **Custom Reports** — Schedule automated daily, weekly, or monthly fleet reports

## How It Works

1. Install the Trackora GPS device on each vehicle (under 30 minutes per unit)
2. Log in to the Trackora web or mobile dashboard
3. Your entire fleet appears live on the map within minutes
