## Overview

Video Telematics combines AI-powered cameras with Trackora's telematics platform to give you eyes inside and outside every vehicle — in real time.

## Coming Soon

This module is currently in active development and will launch alongside our ADAS hardware line.

## Planned Features

- **Dual 4K cameras** — Forward-facing road camera + inward-facing driver camera
- **AI event detection** — Automatic flagging of harsh braking, lane departure, tailgating, fatigue
- **Live streaming** — View any vehicle's camera feed from the dashboard
- **Clip export** — Download 30-second event clips for insurance or HR purposes
- **Driver coaching** — Automated post-trip review with scored event breakdown
