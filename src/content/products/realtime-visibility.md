## Overview

The Control Tower gives fleet operators a bird's-eye view of all vehicles, drivers, and assets — live, at any scale. It is designed for logistics managers who need to make fast decisions across large, complex fleets.

## Key Features

- **Live Map Dashboard** — See every vehicle's location, speed, and status in real time
- **Alert Centre** — Configurable alerts for speeding, geo-fence breaches, harsh braking, and idling
- **Multi-Fleet Support** — Manage multiple depots or business units from one account
- **Driver Scorecards** — Automatic scoring on acceleration, braking, cornering, and speed
- **Custom KPI Widgets** — Pin the metrics that matter most to your operations team

## Use Cases

### Long-Haul Logistics
Track cross-country shipments with ETA predictions and delay alerts sent directly to your dispatch team.

### Urban Delivery Fleets
Monitor dense urban routes with stop-by-stop visibility and real-time rerouting suggestions.

### Enterprise Compliance
Generate automated compliance and duty-of-care reports for corporate fleet audits.

## Technical Specs

- Data refresh: **5 seconds**
- Map accuracy: **< 2.5 metres**
- Alert delivery: **< 3 seconds** via push, SMS, or email
- Supported devices: All Trackora hardware + major third-party OBD-II trackers
