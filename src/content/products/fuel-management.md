## Overview

Trackora's Fuel Management System pairs IoT fuel rod sensors with cloud analytics to give you complete fuel transparency — independent of the driver or vehicle ECU.

## Planned Features

- **Litre-level tracking** — Accurate to ±0.5% regardless of vehicle type
- **Drain detection** — Instant SMS/push alert on abnormal fuel drops
- **Per-trip cost** — Automatic fuel cost attribution to each trip and driver
- **Tank fill detection** — Confirm fuelling events and quantities in real time
- **Theft reports** — Historical drain pattern analysis for insurance claims
